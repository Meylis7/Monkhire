What is the Monkhire DigiTech Contractor Network?

The Monkhire DigiTech Contractor Network is an exclusive network of senior developers, data scientists, designers, product managers and SDETs, that have spent several years at top software companies like Google, Microsoft, Netflix, or Amazon, but have made the switch to contracting.

As a member, we provide you with community, support, and most importantly - work. Monkhire DigiTech takes care of finding work, managing the account, and collecting payments - all the stuff that makes contracting suck.

How it works

The Monkhire DigiTech Network is a marketplace for hiring senior remote developers located in Central and South America. You get senior developers - with excellent communication skills - that work when you do.

Submit your application

First, submit a Monkhire DigiTech member application. In it you'll tell us about your work history and provide your areas of expertise.

We review your application

We check to make sure you meet the bar. Read more about our membership requirements

Join the community

Join our Slack group, meet other members, ask questions, share your knowledge, come to meetups and find new opportunities.

Get matched to work opportunities

When we find a project that matches your skill set and experience, we'll send you an email describing the project in detail. If you're interested, simply reply to the email and we'll start the process.

Membership Requirements

Being a contractor can be hard

Let's face it, there are some things about being a contractor that just suck. Worrying about your next job, dealing with bad clients, and chasing down payments can be really stressful.

It can also get quite lonely working for yourself. It would be really great if you could get the benefit of working at a larger company, but still have all the freedom, flexibility and independence that makes working for yourself so great. That's exactly why we created Monkhire.

Monkhire DigiTech helps contractors thrive

We do all the heavy lifting of finding work, developing client relationships, managing accounts, billing clients, following up on invoices, processing payments, etc.

We'll also help you find health insurance, setup your LLC and find a good tax accountant. You get to focus on doing what you do best - building great software.
